About

Lupe Salas is a Bilingual producer, songwriter, singer music artist. Born and raised in Buenos Aires, Argentina, she lives in a cyber world worldwide.
Lupe started developing her love for music since a kid, at 8 years old, she knew she was destined to be a music artist, and was going to pursue that dream. She started getting basic knowledge of daws and music production in 2017, in 2018 bought a midi controller with a microphone, but she was still in high school so it wasn't until 2020 when she learnt how to produce music to its fullest by watching YouTube tutorials. In 2 months, she was creating her own songs.
At least for now, she does everything by herself, from the music production, songwriting, recording sessions, to the art covers even music videos, everything is directed by herself.
"I would love to bring something to this world, we all share same feelings, I would like to help people with my music and leave a message for my generation. I don't wanna be another fake artist who talks about depression. I want to talk about real stuff gen z is going thorugh. My experiences through trauma and having a hard childhood give me something to talk about. I hope my music helps you, just like it helped me" - Lupe
